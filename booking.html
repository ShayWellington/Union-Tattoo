Booking Info

Walk Ins

We do walk in tattoos on the day whenever we can, the best thing to do is come into the shop for a quick chat and check our availability.

Booking a Consultation

We do not price tattoos by telephone or email. You must book a consultation to come into the shop to discuss your idea. Sizing, detailing and placement all play factors in this. We charge per piece for individual tattoos and per hour for large scale, ongoing work. Scroll down to book a consultation.

Age Limit

16 years old is the youngest we will tattoo, any client under 18 must come in with a parent or guardian and both provide valid photo ID in order to complete a parental consent form.
